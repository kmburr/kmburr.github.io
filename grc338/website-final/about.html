Kristen’s Cellar Estates is a celebration of Greek winemaking traditions, rooted in centuries of history and crafted with modern artistry. Founded by Kristen Burr, whose heritage hails from the sun-kissed vineyards of Greece, our winery blends the ancient art of viticulture with a commitment to sustainability and innovation. Drawing inspiration from the Greek legacy of Oenotria, the land of wine, we cultivate our vines with care, ensuring every bottle reflects the unique character of our terroir.

At Kristen’s Cellar, each wine is a tribute to the beauty and richness of Greek winemaking. From bold reds to crisp whites, our wines are handpicked and crafted to evoke the timeless essence of Greece. With a nod to the ancient Greek word Kallos—meaning beauty—our wines embody elegance, passion, and a deep respect for the land. Whether you’re enjoying a glass of our Kallos Reserve Cabernet Sauvignon or a refreshing Nostos Chardonnay, every sip takes you on a journey through history, flavor, and heritage.
